# 1. Purpose

The Repository Overview introduces FlashRides OS by defining its purpose, scope, objectives, and guiding principles.

It provides a common understanding of FlashRides OS before readers begin working with the repository's governance, standards, operational documentation, business knowledge, technical information, and supporting artifacts.

This document serves as the conceptual introduction to the repository and does not define implementation requirements, repository structure, or navigation rules.

---

# 2. Scope

This document applies to the FlashRides OS repository as a whole.

It describes the overall purpose of the repository and establishes the principles upon which repository governance, organization, and maintenance are based.

Detailed requirements are defined within the applicable standards, policies, procedures, and governance artifacts.

---

# 3. What is FlashRides OS?

FlashRides OS is the authoritative operating system for the FlashRides organization.

It provides the controlled repository of governance, business knowledge, operational processes, technical standards, reference information, and supporting artifacts required to establish, operate, maintain, and continually improve the organization.

FlashRides OS is intended to ensure that organizational knowledge is:

- structured;
- controlled;
- discoverable;
- maintainable;
- reusable; and
- continuously improved throughout the lifecycle of the organization.

Rather than functioning solely as a document repository, FlashRides OS serves as the authoritative source of organizational knowledge and operational guidance.

---

# 4. Mission

The mission of FlashRides OS is to provide a single, authoritative, and well-governed repository that enables the organization to operate consistently, efficiently, safely, and sustainably.

The repository supports informed decision-making, operational excellence, knowledge preservation, regulatory compliance, continuous improvement, and organizational scalability.

---

# 5. Repository Objectives

FlashRides OS is intended to:

- establish a single source of truth for controlled organizational knowledge;
- preserve organizational knowledge independently of individual personnel;
- support consistent decision-making throughout the organization;
- improve operational efficiency through standardized documentation;
- support governance and controlled change;
- facilitate training and onboarding;
- improve discoverability of information;
- promote consistency across all organizational artifacts; and
- support long-term organizational growth.

---

# 6. Core Principles

FlashRides OS is founded upon the following principles:

- FlashRides OS serves as the single authoritative source for controlled organizational knowledge.
- Every controlled artifact exists for a defined purpose.
- Repository organization shall promote discoverability, consistency, and long-term maintainability.
- Governance, standards, procedures, reference material, and operational knowledge shall be maintained as separate but related artifact types.
- Approved artifacts describe the current approved baseline of FlashRides OS.
- Repository improvements shall be introduced through controlled change rather than undocumented modification.
- Organizational knowledge shall be maintained as an organizational asset rather than individual knowledge.

These principles guide the development and maintenance of the repository and support consistent repository governance.
