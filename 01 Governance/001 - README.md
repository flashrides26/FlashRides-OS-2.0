# 1. Purpose

This section establishes the governance framework for FlashRides.

It defines how the organization is directed, managed, and controlled through its governing principles, policies, standards, delegated authorities, and governance records.

The Governance section provides the authoritative foundation upon which all business, operational, technical, and administrative activities are based.

---

# 2. Scope

This section applies to the governance of the FlashRides organization.

It defines the organization's governing framework but does not prescribe the detailed operational activities performed by individual business functions.

Operational requirements are maintained within their respective repository sections.

---

# 3. Objectives

The objectives of this section are to:

- establish the constitutional foundation of FlashRides;
- define the organization's governance philosophy;
- document mandatory organizational policies;
- establish organizational standards;
- define authority and decision-making responsibilities;
- maintain governance records and registers; and
- support accountability, consistency, and continual improvement.

---

# 4. Section Organization

The Governance section is organized into the following areas.

## Constitution

Defines the organization's foundational governing artifacts, including its identity, purpose, guiding principles, and governance framework.

## Policies

Contains mandatory organizational policies that establish management direction and organizational expectations.

## Standards

Defines organizational standards that support consistent implementation of approved policies.

## Authority and Delegation

Defines organizational authority, delegated responsibilities, approval authorities, and decision-making responsibilities.

## Governance Registers

Maintains controlled governance records, inventories, and registers supporting organizational governance.

---

# 5. Governance Principles

Governance within FlashRides is based on the following principles:

- accountability;
- transparency;
- integrity;
- consistency;
- stewardship;
- controlled change;
- continual improvement; and
- compliance with approved organizational requirements.

---

# 6. Relationship to Other Repository Sections

This section provides governance for the entire organization.

Other repository sections shall align with the governance established here.

This section governs organizational direction but does not duplicate the detailed requirements maintained within Business, Operations, Technology, People, Fleet, Safety, Customer, Projects, Shared Resources, or Repository Governance.
